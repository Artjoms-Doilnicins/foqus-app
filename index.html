Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

Is FoqusApp free?

Yes! It's 100% free — no subscriptions, ads, or sign-ups.

Does it require an internet connection?

No. FoqusApp works fully offline, and all your data is stored locally.

Is there a mobile version?

Not yet. FoqusApp is currently desktop-only, but mobile support is planned.

Where is my data stored?

All your information is saved locally on your device. We don’t collect or sync anything.
